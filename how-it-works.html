How It Works

1 - The website's goal: The goal of the site is to serve as a disclosure free for banners with different sizes of sites and companies from various branches, on the other hand each site or company that publishes on MyBanners disclose our banner on your site thus increasing popularity of MyBanners site in turn increasing the chances of raising visitors to your site.

2 - How it works:
  1. You send us your banner to insert into our database (Read Terms of Use and Privacy Policy).
    It is possible publish more than one banner but you should send one at a time.
  2. Your banner will be displayed on our homepage (The banners are arranged randomly and changed every 7 seconds dynamically to all banners be displayed).
  3. You put our banner on your site.
  4. Promote your site and our site for your family and friends through social networks.
3 - Banners accepted sizes:
  • 125x125
  • 300x250
  • 468x60
